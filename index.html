The Swimmer's Challenge

Frequent aquatic exposure severely impacts dermatological health. SPLASH formulates specifically for these extreme parameters.

Environmental Stressors

  • Chlorine & Halogens Strips natural sebum, alters skin pH, and degrades hair protein structure.
  • Saltwater Exposure Osmotic stress draws moisture out of the epidermis, drastically increasing TEWL (Trans-Epidermal Water Loss).
  • UV Radiation Accelerated photo-aging exacerbated by water reflection.
  • Mechanical Stress Friction from goggles, caps, and constant towel drying compromises the stratum corneum.

Formulation Strategy

Our approach mitigates these stressors through a targeted sequential methodology designed by cosmetic chemists.
